About Us

Animal Care Oman is a community-based platform dedicated to supporting pet owners across Oman. Our mission is to provide reliable information and resources to help ensure that pets receive the care they deserve.

As a non-profit initiative, we aim to bridge the gap between pet owners and essential veterinary services. We’re committed to promoting responsible pet ownership and creating a trusted space for pet owners to find answers and connect with the right care.

Our platform offers a range of resources, including a veterinary directory, expert pet care tips, and a veterinarian AI chatbot, all designed to support the well-being of pets throughout Oman.
